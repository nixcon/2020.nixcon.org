  • Q. How will Q&A work?

    Join #nixcon on either IRC or Discord to ask questions. The rooms are bridged. The moderator (nbathum) will feed questions to the emcee (worldofpeace).


    Folks in chat should be welcoming to everyone. If you can answer someone's question completely please do so. The moderator will filter questions during the Q&A period. Good questions are relevant and meaningful for the speaker and related to the content in the talk. Questions which are off-topic or not within community standards will not be forwarded.
